about us

Oncoor is a marketing agency bridging the gap between athletes and brands. Oncoor represents more than 65 athletes across professional, collegiate and Olympic sports.

Oncoor was founded in 2017 by Brian Fagan. Since then, Oncoor has emerged as one of the country’s largest sports marketing firms. In July of 2021 the NCAA announced new guidelines allowing collegiate athletes to take advantage of their Name Image and Likeness. These changes have put Oncoor at the forefront of the industry as the country’s largest NIL agency.
